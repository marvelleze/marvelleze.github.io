About

The Person
Behind the Lens

I'm Marvellous Ezekwem, a Cinematographer and Video Editor based in Lagos, Nigeria, working under the creative identity MESI (Marvell Eze Shot It). I tell human-centered stories through intentional light, composition, and movement, across commercial productions, short films, documentaries, and branded content.

With hands-on experience spanning TV commercials, real estate campaigns, web series, and visual poetry, I bring both technical precision and creative instinct to every frame. From pre-production planning through to final cut. Currently pursuing a BSc in Film Production at NOUN, and always in pursuit of the next story worth telling.
